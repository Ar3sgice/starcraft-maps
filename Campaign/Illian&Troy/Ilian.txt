* Play Instructions and Installation *

Unzip into the .....\Starcraft\maps\. To play, select Single Player, Play Custom and 
select "(1)Ilian1.scm". Select "Use Map Settings" then click on Ok.

* Play Information *

Mission type		: Single Player Campaign
Difficulty rating	: medium
New WAVs		: No
New Cutscenes		: No

* Additional Info *

This campaign contains eleven maps.  There are four possible paths through the 
campaign as shown below-
1-2-3-4-5-6a-7a
1-2-3-4-5-6a-7b-8
1-2-3-4-5-6b-7b-8
1-2-3-4-5-6c
a is when you ally with the Zerg, b with the Protoss.
